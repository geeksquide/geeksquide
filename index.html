How to Schedule a Geek Squad Appointment

How to Book Your Geek Squad Appointment

  1. Visit the Geek Squad Scheduling Page: Go to the official Geek Squad booking portal on Best Buy’s website.
  2. Select a Service: Choose from various options like tech support, virus removal, TV mounting, and more.
  3. Enter Your Location: Provide your ZIP code to find the nearest available Geek Squad locations for in-home or in-store appointments.
  4. Pick Your Appointment Type: Choose between in-home, in-store, or remote services, depending on your needs.
  5. Choose Date & Time: Browse through available time slots and select the most convenient one for your schedule.
  6. Provide Device Details: Give a brief description of your issue and any relevant device information to help the techs prepare.
  7. Submit Your Information: Enter your contact details (email, phone number) to confirm your booking.
  8. Confirm & Receive Confirmation: After reviewing all details, submit the form. You’ll get a confirmation code and appointment reminder via email/SMS.
Tip: Have your device model numbers, error codes, or issue descriptions ready to streamline the process.

Manage Your Appointment

If you need to reschedule or cancel your Geek Squad appointment, follow these simple steps:

  1. Log in to the Geek Squad portal using your confirmation code.
  2. Select the appointment you want to manage (reschedule or cancel).
  3. Choose a new date or time or confirm your cancellation.
  4. You'll receive an updated confirmation email or SMS.
Ensure to cancel or reschedule well in advance to avoid delays and allow others to book open slots.
